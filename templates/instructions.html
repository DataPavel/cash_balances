Follow the steps below to learn how to use this app



Step 1

By clicking on the How to link on the navigation bar you go to this page, where you can find instructions about how to use this application

Step 2

By clicking on the Initial Setup link on the navigation bar you go to Initial Setup page, where you can find two bottons: Companies and Currencies. There you can setup and delete companies and currencies by completing the associated forms

Step 3

By clicking on the Accounts link on the navigation bar you go to the page where you can setup bank accounts used by the companies

Step 4

By clicking on the Balances link on the navigation bar you go to the page where you can enter bank balances at a particular date

Step 5

By clicking on the Cash Balances link on the navigation bar you can find dashboard. There you can setup a filter and download the resulting table in xlsx format
